# Travel Archive Website Plan

## 1. Project Overview

### Project Concept

A photo-based travel magazine platform that archives personal travel
experiences and introduces meaningful places around the world.

This platform combines: - Travel storytelling - Original photography -
Local recommendations - Community sharing - Photo-based merchandise

The goal is to transform travel memories into valuable content and
physical products such as postcards, prints, and lifestyle goods.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. Vision & Goal

## Vision

"Turning travel moments into stories that can be remembered and shared."

## Main Goals

1.  Archive personal travel memories
2.  Provide authentic travel inspiration
3.  Discover hidden local experiences
4.  Build a travel community
5.  Expand travel photography into merchandise

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 3. Core Identity

## Brand Positioning

Not a typical travel guide.

Instead of: "Top 10 places to visit"

Focus on: "Places worth remembering"

The website delivers: - Personal experiences - Emotional storytelling -
Original photography - Local discoveries

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 4. Target Audience

People who love traveling and discovering new places.

Including: - Travelers planning trips - Photography lovers - Food
explorers - People interested in travel memories

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 5. Differentiation Strategy

## Hidden Local Experiences

Focus on places that are not obvious tourist attractions.

Examples: - Small local restaurants - Neighborhood cafes - Quiet
streets - Unique cultural experiences

Content direction:

Not: "Best restaurant in Tokyo"

But: "A small ramen shop discovered while walking through Tokyo streets"

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Story-Based Travel Content

Each location includes: - Why I visited - What I felt - What makes this
place special - Practical information

Structure:

Photo → Story → Information → Recommendation

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Photo as Main Content

Photography is the core asset.

The website should prioritize: - Large images - Minimal text layout -
Magazine-style presentation

Photos can later become: - Postcards - Posters - Frames - Limited
collections

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 6. Website Structure

## Home

Purpose: Create first impression and introduce the world of the brand.

Main Feature: Interactive 3D Globe

User Experience: 1. User sees rotating globe 2. Selects a country 3.
Travel memories appear 4. Opens magazine article

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 7. Main Pages

## Magazine

Categories: - Destination - Hidden Gems - Food - Stories

## World Map

Features: - Visited locations - Photo pins - Travel memories

## Postcard Shop

Products: - Photo postcards - Art prints - Frames - Travel collections

Concept: "Bring a travel moment home"

## Community

Users can: - Upload travel photos - Share recommendations - Save
places - Create travel collections

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 8. Design Direction

## Style Keyword

Apple + Travel Magazine + Minimal

Design Principles: - White space - Large typography - High-quality
images - Elegant interactions

Avoid: - Heavy animations - Complex UI

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 9. User Journey

First Visit:

Home ↓ Interactive Globe ↓ Choose Destination ↓ Magazine Article ↓
Discover Place ↓ Purchase Postcard

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 10. Content Format

Article Template:

Title: Short emotional title

Hero Image: Large travel photo

Story: Personal experience

Location Info: - Location - Best time - How to visit - Recommendation

Gallery: Photo collection

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 11. Monetization Plan

Phase 1: Build audience

Focus: - Travel articles - Photography archive - Community

Phase 2: Product launch

Products: - Postcards - Prints - Frames

Phase 3: Expansion

Possible: - Travel guide products - Brand partnerships - Creator
marketplace

------------------------------------------------------------------------

A travel platform where places become stories, photos become memories,
